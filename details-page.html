How to download the Shopkite app

To download the shopkite App follow the steps below:

• Go to your app store, either “Play Store” if you are using an android phone or “App Store” if you are using an iphone.
• Search for Shopkite(one word), it is the orange logo with the white kite.
• Tap on it to download.

Once your download is complete, you are ready to sign up!
